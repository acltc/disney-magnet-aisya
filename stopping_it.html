What You Can Do To Stop Animal Cruelty

-Signs Of Animal Cruelty:

  • Very thin animals
  • Open sores
  • Extreme loss of hair
  • Fear of humans
  • Extremely dirty
  • Heavy chains around the neck
  • Dehydration
  • Weak
  • Unable to walk or function properly

-Adopt:

Instead of purchasing your animal from a puppymill, breeder, or petstore adopt your animal from your local animal shelter. There are many furry friends that have been abandoned and abused, and are in need of a loving home.
